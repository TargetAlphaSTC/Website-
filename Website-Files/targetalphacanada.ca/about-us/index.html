ABOUT TARGET ALPHA

At Target Alpha, we strive to lead the revolution in secondary school student’s financial education. Our team has launched specially-designed investment clubs throughout high schools across Canada and will enlist these groups in a variety of competitions and events throughout the school year. We believe that each club is unique and should be operated in their own, professional way. Customize your club the way you see fit! Schools can express their interest in joining Target Alpha at any time, but official registration will begin in September and end in early October.

We have provincial boards in Ontario, British Columbia and Alberta. Our head office is located in Toronto, Ontario.

For more information, don’t hesitate to reach out to us on our Facebook page or visit Contact Us to send us an email! We would love to talk.
