WELCOME TO TARGET ALPHA.

We strive to offer Canadian secondary students remarkable opportunities in business, investments, and networking, all while improving their confidence and communication abilities.

BENEFITS OF TARGET ALPHA

NETWORKING

Make connections with driven, like-minded secondary students and business professionals from across the country at our exclusive events and conferences.

RESOURCES

Learn from the information packages delivered to each school chapter, as well as the comprehensive 16-page definitions document prepared by our team.

LEADERSHIP

Gain the opportunity to give presentations at our conferences, help to lead crowds and groups at educational events, increase exposure, and build confidence.

EDUCATION

Nowadays, everyone wants to invest in the stock market, but many simply don’t know how. Learn with us and become the positive anomaly!

RECOGNITION

During admissions, post-secondary institutions give special preference to students who involve themselves in organizations such as Target Alpha S.T.C.

AWARDS

Receive awards based on dedication and performance in our real-time competitions and simulations, as well as raffled off gifts at our events and conferences.

COMPETITION

For the first time, your school chapter is able to face against others from across Canada, in our real-time stock trading challenge; an opportunity to demonstrate your skills.

PERFORMANCE

Equipped with the knowledge you gained from Target Alpha, excel in business and finance related courses and gain an overall motivation for academic success.

EVEN MORE

The Target Alpha National Board is constantly improving the resources we have to offer and strives to offer an even wider array of opportunities to students.
