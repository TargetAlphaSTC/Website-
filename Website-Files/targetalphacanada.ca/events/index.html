AlphaHacks: FinTech Hackathon @ Wealthsimple

With trillions of dollars up for grabs, fintech aims to provide tech solutions to problems in the financial services industry and Toronto’s newest hackathon. AlphaHacks will help students do just that.

What is it?

Hosted by Target Alpha STC, Canada’s leading youth literacy NPO, AlphaHacks is an intimate, high-value hackathon for those interested in technology and/or finance.

Who will be attending?

We will be inviting 75 engineers, designers and business students to “hack” solutions to problems in the finance world.

How can I apply?

Applications to attend the hackathon will open on October 8th.

When is it?

The current date of the hackathon is Saturday, November 25th.

Where is it?

The event will be held at Wealthsimple’s headquarters.
